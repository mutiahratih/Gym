OUR FACILITIES

We helps you get the most out of your workout and gym experience like an Idol.

Pulldown

The first tool or machine that you can find at our gym is the Lat pulldown machine. The movement that can be done is to pull the lever up and down. The load on the tool can be adjusted according to your strength or needs.

Rowing

Through this tool you can train your leg and abdominal muscles. To use this tool you need to sit down first, then place your feet on the pedals. Finally, you just need to pull and release the rope or handle like rowing.

Smith

Next up is the smith machine. This machine allows you to do a lot of movements such as lifting weights while lying down or squats. In addition, at the top of the tool there is a handle for pull ups.

Cable

On the cable machine, you can train your hand muscles by pulling the cable that is locked on the left and right. You can use this tool by pulling once two ropes on the left and right, or just focus on one side.



Squat Rack

By using this tool you can do squats while lifting weights. However, you have to adjust the weight you lift. Squat rack allows you to be able to increase the load according to your maximum ability in any exercise movement using a barbell.

Peck Deck

The pec deck machine helps your arms to be more active. This tool will help tighten the muscles of the hands, chest and abdomen. You can adjust the weight you lift with this tool.

Treadmill

The treadmill allows a user to walk or run in place on the machine on a belt that moves underneath the feet in a continuous loop. The walker or runner sets the belt to a desired speed, usually measured in miles per hour or kilometers per hour.

Elliptical

The elliptical machine allows you to get a good aerobic workout, which can strengthen your heart, lungs, and muscles. This, in turn, can help build your stamina and endurance.
